Our services

The food

Do not worry about the food, our home chefs work with the best local suppliers and will be in charge of providing the ingredients of the menu.
The menus are imagined by our chefs, but can of course be ajusted according to your tastes, allergies or special wishes.

The preparation

The chef brings the necessary equipment and prepares the dishes in your kitchen. In order to be ready at the time of the meal that you will have determined with him, he arrives on average between 1h30 and 2h before. All you have to do is briefly show him where the utensils and cutlery are, he will adapt and start cooking.

The table service

The chef is responsible for setting the plates and serving them. A chef can serve up to 10 people. Beyond that, he will be accompanied by a waiter or maître d'hôtel as he will be busy in the kitchen to be ready at the time you have agreed.

Tidying up

Chefs are used to leaving the kitchen as clean as when they arrived. When he leaves, the dishes are done (dishwasher or hand-washing according to your instructions), and all you have to do is enjoy your time with your guests.

The transportation

The chef's travel expenses are included.

Our chefs

Bernard Houx

In the world of gastronomy for 25 years, Chef Bernard Houx has had the privilege of working in great palaces, such as the Concorde chain, the Intercontinental chain or even Mr Paul Bocuse's. He also had the chance to open his own restaurant and to develop various concepts for others. After this rich and varied career, he now wishes to share his cuisine and a more intimate relationship with his guests. He describes his cuisine as classic or modern, the latter depending on the wishes of the guests. But it is above all a generous and gourmet cuisine!

Amaury Tellier

Self-taught, Chef Amaury Tellier was trained in gastronomy by his mother, his aunts and his grandmother, whom he loved to assist: they provided him with a wealth of knowledge and tricks of the trade. Today, Chef Tellier cooks with products raised and cultivated by the expert hands of local market gardeners and breeders. He sources his fruits and vegetables, fish and spices on a daily basis, with the knowledge of good producers. He knows how to bring the spirit of an innovative, light and inspired cuisine to his guests' table.

Ilaria Del Rossia

Originally from Italy, it was while growing up among the sweet smells of Italian specialties that Chef Del Rossia decided to start cooking. Cooking has always been a great passion. As her family used to remind her, using fresh products is essential to bring out the flavours! She is therefore particularly attached to the quality of the raw materials, their origin, but also to the way of working them, the dressing and the final presentation. Ilaria is particularly attached to her region and will not fail to make you discover an explosion of southern flavours.
